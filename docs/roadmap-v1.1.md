# ECG Lab v1.1 Roadmap

## Purpose

ECG Lab v1.1 is focused on improving the educational experience of the existing
Lead II ECG simulator. The priority is helping learners understand why a rhythm
looks the way it does, without expanding the app into diagnosis, treatment
guidance, 12-lead ECG generation, or vectorcardiography.

## Planned Improvements

- Strengthen the explanation text for all 12 existing rhythm cases.
- Show structured learning points in Learning mode:
  - key findings
  - recognition tips
  - common pitfalls
  - clinical notes
- Add post-answer Quiz feedback:
  - correct answer
  - why this rhythm
  - key recognition points
- Keep language education-focused and avoid clinical decision-support wording.
- Extend validation so missing education fields are detected before release.

## Medical Review

All educational copy should be reviewed by a medically qualified reviewer before
clinical teaching use. ECG Lab remains a learning simulator and does not replace
clinical diagnosis, emergency response, treatment decisions, patient monitoring,
or medical device output.

## Out of Scope for v1.1

- New rhythm cases.
- 12-lead ECG waveform generation.
- Clinical vectorcardiography.
- Major ECG waveform engine refactors.
- Full UI redesign.
- Authentication, databases, or user accounts.
- AI diagnosis or clinical decision-support features.
